How are fires dangerous?


Fire Prevention tips:

Be mindful of flammable items

Objects such as matches, lighters, and candles are flammable objects. Keep flammable items that are out of children's reach. Also, be mindful to keep flammable liquids in a well-ventilated area that is away from potential sources of ignition. So flammable objects should not be stored near electrical equipment or heating equipment.

Unplug appliances that are not in use

Unplug appliances that are not in use. Also, be mindful not to plug in a lot of appliances at the same time. Avoid octopus connections or wires tangling each other. If some appliances are needed to be plugged on always, check that if you leave them plugged on, they are in a good condition.


Be Responsible when cooking

When you are cooking, make sure to not leave the kitchen. Always watch the food that you are cooking. If you need to leave the kitchen from time to time make sure someone will watch it for you or use a timer that will alert you when the food is already cooked. Cooking at home is one of the most common reasons for fire so be responsible when cooking.


Install a smoke detector and have a fire extinguisher

Install a Smoke detector in your home to better sense if there is smoke in your house. Accidents are hard to avoid so it is better to be prepared than sorry. Also, have a fire extinguisher in your home so if said accidents appeared, you are ready to handle the situation.
